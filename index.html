Featured Memorial

Eleanor Grace Johnson

January 5, 1942 - July 15, 2023

Eleanor Grace Johnson, 81, of Oakwood, passed away on July 15, 2023. She was a beloved wife, mother, grandmother, and great-grandmother who dedicated her life to her family and community.

Eleanor was an active volunteer at the local food bank for over 20 years and was known for her incredible baking skills, especially her famous apple pie that won first place at the county fair for five consecutive years.

She is survived by her husband of 58 years, Charles; three children; seven grandchildren; and two great-grandchildren. A celebration of life will be held on July 25, 2023 at Oakwood Community Church.
